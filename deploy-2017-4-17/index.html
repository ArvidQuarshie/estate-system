About

Welcome to BuruBuru , a small surburb town found about 20 minutes drive from the capital city of Nairobi. It is comprised of different estates from 1 to 5. It is also a business hub that houses a library , banks , hospitals and schools.

 

BURU-BURU WELFARE GROUP

This is a group that is in charge of ensuring that all the needs of Buru-Buru are met . This includes services such as organizing events , rehabilitation of infrastructure and pollicy development.

BURU-BURU TRADERS ASSOCIATION

This group involves all business owners within Buru Buru. It is designed to help them benefit from business opportunities that might arise within the estate. Also, it helps them to develop the economic hub that is at the center of the estate.

 

Mission

Our mission is to facilitate peaceful and reliable environment for all the residents of BuruBuru.
